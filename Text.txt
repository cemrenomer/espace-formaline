About
one gee in fog is an artist-run space committed to exploring the entanglements of art and literature, and to promoting young cultural practitioners engaging with visual arts, writing and research by the means of the arts. one gee in fog includes an exhibition space, a residency, an online platform named two gees in eggs, and a library and publishing platform called three gees in luggage.

founded in 2014 in chêne-bourg (geneva) by ceel mogami de haas and lucas cantori, one gee in fog has moved to geneva in a new space shared with éditions clinamen. since 2024, the annual programmation of the space is in charge of a curatorial team composed by erell le pape, mina achermann and tara ulmann.


Initié par le collectif HOY (Camille Kaiser, Vicente Lesser, Julie Marmet), Espace 3353 a ouvert en octobre 2018. La programmation inclut différentes pratiques et médiums artistiques et de recherche, encourageant en particulier les démarches collectives, engagées et expérimentales. Le collectif HOY s’engage pour la rémunération des artistes en versant un cachet à chacun·e·x des artistes avec lesquels·les·x il collabore.
